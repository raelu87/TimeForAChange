CHANGE

In school bullying, the public tends to pay attention to the two groups of the bully and the victim, while ignoring the importance of bystanders in this situation.However, bystanders account for the most significant proportion and the largest number of people in school bullying incidents.

When school bullying occurs, bystanders ignore the situation, pass the responsibility on others, take no action, and let the bullying incident develop or leave the bullying scene directly. The indifferent behaviour of such bystanders may indirectly increase the number of bullying, or it may lead to aggravation of bullies, and indirectly promote the occurrence of bullying. Therefore, during my anti-bully activities concerning in junior high school, the purpose of my design is to make bystanders change their attitudes towards bullying on school into positive bystanders, that is, protectors.
